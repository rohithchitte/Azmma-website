About AZMMA

AZMMA is revolutionizing the way Indians think about tea. As the creator of India's first protein tea powder, we've combined the country's love for tea with the nutritional benefits of high-quality protein.

Our journey began with a simple idea: create a beverage that's not only delicious but also contributes to your daily health and fitness goals. After years of research and development, we're proud to offer a product that delivers on taste, nutrition, and convenience.

Made from 100% organic ingredients and manufactured in Nellore, India, AZMMA Protein Tea Powder is carefully crafted to support your wellness journey. Whether you're looking to manage your weight, improve digestion, or simply incorporate more protein into your diet, our tea powder is the perfect solution.
